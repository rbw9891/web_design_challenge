Is There a Relationship Between Latitude & Temperature?

As one could imagine, there is a pretty distinct relationship between temperature
and latitude. The farther one gets from the equator, the colder the temperatures and
vice versa. It is interesting to note that extreme cold temperatures are much more present
in the northern hemisphere as compared to the southern. This could have something to do
with the fact that much more of the human population lives in the north as compared to the
south and thus we have less data points for southern cities. This is also have something
to do with much larger land masses farther towards the north pole than towards the south pole.
There just aren't a lot of places extrememly south of the equator for humans to live!
