Is There a Relationship Between Latitude & Wind Speed?

Once again, there doesn't seem to be a distinct relationship between wind speed
and latitude. The main theme one could glean from this visualization is that
there are very few places within the sample set which have high wind speeds,
at least during the day and time when the data was retrieved from the OpenWeatherMap
API.
