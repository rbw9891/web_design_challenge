Is There a Relationship Between Latitude & Humidity?

There doesn't seem to be a distinct relationship between latitude and
humidity. On the other hand, from this visualization it is interesting to
see that there are many more humid places than dry ones from this random sample
set. A larger sample might be needed to determine if this is true. It is also
worth noting that the data points with the lowest humidity are approximately around
30 degrees latitude, which is a widely known region for containing many of the
world's largest deserts.
